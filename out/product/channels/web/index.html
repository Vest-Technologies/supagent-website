Integration Process

Follow these simple steps to integrate our AI agent with your website

Create Your Agent

Design and train your AI agent with your knowledge base and brand voice.

Configure Your Widget

Customize the appearance and behavior of your chat widget to match your website.

Add the Integration Code

Insert a simple JavaScript snippet into your website to activate the agent.

Test and Refine

Validate the integration and fine-tune your agent's responses.

Monitor Performance

Track engagement metrics and continuously improve your agent.

Sample Integration Code

<script
  src="https://chatbot-content-public.s3.eu-north-1.amazonaws.com/general/supagent.umd.js?v=1.0.0"
  data-id="{agentId}"
  id="supagent-script"
  async defer>
</script>
Copy and paste this code snippet just before the closing </body> tag of your website.
