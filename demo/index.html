FAQ

AI Agents are intelligent virtual assistants powered by advanced artificial intelligence that can handle customer inquiries, provide support, and engage with users across multiple channels. They can be trained on your specific knowledge base to represent your brand accurately.

AI Agents handle routine inquiries and tasks autonomously, but they're designed to seamlessly escalate complex issues to human agents when needed. This hybrid approach ensures customers always receive the appropriate level of support while maximizing your team's efficiency.

You can customize your AI Agent's appearance, voice, personality, and knowledge base to align with your brand identity. Our platform provides intuitive tools for training your agent on your specific products, services, and company information.

Our AI Agents can be deployed across multiple channels including web chat, WhatsApp, Instagram, Facebook Messenger, Slack, phone systems, and custom mobile applications. This omnichannel approach ensures consistent customer experiences regardless of how they choose to engage.
